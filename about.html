Prime Minister's Special Scholarship Scheme

The Prime Minister's Special Scholarship Scheme (PMSSS) was launched by the Ministry of Education, Government of India, to provide financial assistance to the students from Jammu & Kashmir and Ladakh to pursue higher education outside their home regions.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is eligible for PMSSS?

Students who are domiciled in J&K/Ladakh, have passed 12th with minimum 60% marks, and whose family income is less than ‚Çπ8 lakhs per annum are eligible.

What courses are covered?

The scheme covers General Degree, Engineering, Medical, Dental, and other professional courses in AICTE/UGC approved institutions.

How do I apply?

Visit the PMSSS portal, register with your details, fill the application form, upload required documents, and submit the application.

When will I receive the scholarship?

After verification and approval, the scholarship amount is directly credited to the student's bank account within 45-60 days.

Can I renew my scholarship?

Yes, the scholarship is renewable annually subject to satisfactory academic performance and compliance with scheme guidelines.
