Apply for Scholarship

Complete the application process in simple steps

Eligibility Criteria

📚

Academic Qualification

Must have passed 12th class examination with minimum 60% marks

🎓

Course

Admission in General Degree, Engineering, Medical, or Professional courses

💰

Family Income

Annual family income should not exceed ₹8 Lakhs per annum

📍

Domicile

Permanent resident of Jammu & Kashmir or Ladakh

Application Process

1

Registration

Create your account with basic details and verify your email/mobile

2

Fill Application

Complete personal, academic, and contact information in the application form

3

Upload Documents

Upload required documents including marksheets, domicile certificate, and income proof

4

Submit & Track

Review and submit your application. Track status through your dashboard

Documents Required

📄 10th Class Marksheet
📄 12th Class Marksheet
📄 Domicile Certificate
📄 Income Certificate
📄 Aadhar Card
📄 Bank Account Details
📄 Passport Size Photograph
📄 Admission Letter (if applicable)
